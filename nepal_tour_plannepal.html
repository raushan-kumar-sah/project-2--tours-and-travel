Best of Nepal Tour is the trip which would be best in a single visit that includes Sightseeing, Short Gentle Hike to Poon Hill and Jungle Safari Experience. This trip Highlights the Spectacular view of Mountain View, Himalayan River, Exploring the World Heritage Site, Jungle Wildlife Safari Experience and the Classic sunrise from Poonhill with trekking experience.

Best of Nepal Tour with Trekking begins after the sightseeing tour in kathmandu valley sightseeing which will provide you a unique experience to learn Nepalese art, culture, tradition, lifestyle and architecture. Next Morning we will drive to Pokhara via optional White Water Rafting in the Himalayan Trishuli River for 3 hours is surely be your amazing experience if you choose. Explore paradise city Pokhara, the best tourist place of Nepal. Roaming around lakeside prepare for trekking we will have boating in fewa lake. The reflection of mountain in phewa lake is surely going to take your breath away. The Four Days treks to Annapurna region will bring you closer to nature and Himalayas. The trek to Ghorepani poonhill will be educational trip to learn culture, lifestyle of Mountain people and hiking experience with sunrise and sunset tour. Then we will ahead towards to Chitwan National Park the wildlife safari tour with Elephant back safari, Canoe ride, Bird watching and many more. Afterward, We will return back to kathmandu giving end to this Nepal Tour and Trekking.

Day 01: Arrival at Kathmandu Airport. Transfer to Hotel and Evening welcom Dinner.

After arrival and customs clearance in kathmandu airport. You’ll be met by the Plan Nepal Tour representative at airport terminal. We would offer you a warm Nepalese welcome and transferred to Hotel. In evening there will be welcome dinner at one of the finest cultural restaurant in nepal which will introduce you to the country's food. Overnight at Standard Hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 02: Full Day SightSeeing in kathmandu Valley.

After breakfast we will have a full day sightseeing of kathmandu valley. It is the best way to explore beautiful temples, stupas, monastries,old royal places and tradition. Explore Bouddhhanath stupa, Kathmandu Durbar Square, Swoyambhunath and Pashupatinath Temple. Overnight at 3 Star Hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 03: Drive from Kathmandu to Pokhara

After Breakfast, Get your bagpack ready to another popular destination. It takes around four to five hours to reach Pokhara. On the way encountering the fast flowing river, green forest and option to take thrilling rafting experience in trishuli river. Pokhara offers magnificent view of snow capped mountain and city view of Pokhara with beautiful lake. The Mount Annapurna, Machhapurchae and other mountain always stand high welcoming every visitor with a smile. You will have oportunity to take Evening walk in the lakeside, truly wonderful. Overnight at Standard Hotel in Pokhara.

Day 04: Drive to Nayapul and Trek to Hile

After breakfast, we will drive for an hour to to Nayapul, a bridge that kicks off our trekking. Following our path we will encounter One the way we will come across the beautiful village, different settlements, waterfalls, Local people and rice fields. Ovenight at Hile.

Day 05: Trek from Hile to Ghorepani (1490m – 2850m).

From the Hile, after a short walk trail crosses the bridge and leads to very steepy stone staircase. it continues to the large village of uleri.From there we can catch the mountain view of Annapurna, Himchuli and Fishtail. After that, we lead through the forest up to Ghorepani. Walking time takes 7 hours.

Day 06: Ghorepani–Poonhill–Ghandrung (2850m–1940m).

At the early morning we will hike from ghorepani to poonhill thorugh the stone staircase. walking time takes 1 hours to the top. From the poonhill we will catch the view of sunrise and white mountains such as haulagiri, Nilgiri, Annapurna , Himchuli, Fishtail, Annapurna IV, and so on. After poonhill, we will trek down to ghorepani from there to tadapani and finally to Ghandrung. Trail leads several time ascending and descending. Walking throughout the forest and village approximately 6 hours.

Day 07: Ghandrung–Nayapul–Pokhara.

Today we will pass through the cultural village ghandrung and trail leads to Nayapul, the route almost flat through the bank of modi river and Several villages. Walking hours take approximately 5 hours, after nayapul we will drive back to pokhara by private vehicle.. Overnight at hotel in Pokhara.

Day 08: Drive Pokhara to Chitwan (160 km 4 Hrs). Jungle Safari Activities.

Today you will drive from pokhara to chitwan by the side you will leave the mountainous city pokhara and drive through the plain terai and on the arrival you will be transferred to the hotel. Later on, Sunset view from Rapti River and Enjoy cultural programme that is performed by Tharu. Overnight at Standard Hotel in Chitwan.

Day 09: Full Day Jungle Safari Activities.

Early in the morning after having breakfast you will ride along the Rapti river in the jungle encountering different types of crocodile. At the time you will visit elephant breeding and jungle activites with elephant back safari. Overnight at Standard Hotel in Chitwan.

Day 10: Transfer Back to Kathmandu from Chitwan. Overnight at Luxurious Hotel in Kathmandu.

After breakfast and Short Jungle Walk transfer back to kathmandu. On the way you will have great opportunity to discover the beautiful fast flowing rivers, forest, hills and Himlayas. It will approximately take 5 hours to reach kathmandu. After that, Free time on your own for shopping, have a relaxing rest on Luxrious Hotel at Kathmandu.

Day 11: Final Transfer to Airport.

Today you have free time until your flight time to Airport. We will exchange our good-byes and now you will be leaving Nepal with all the good memories and unforgettable Experience. We will provide a private transport to International airport for final depature. We thank you for the opportunity of being your travel partner and wish to serve you on your next vacation !
