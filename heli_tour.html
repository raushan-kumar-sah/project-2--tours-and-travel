DAY TOUR OF EVEREST BASE CAMP IN HELICOPTER

Day Tour of Everest Base Camp in Helicopter is the most thrilling adventurous tour to the roof of the world. Discovering the aerial view of the Himalayan range cannot be found anywhere esle in Nepal. Everest Base Camp Helicopter Tour is perfect for those visitors who aren't physically fitness and timely unable to trek in Everest Region. Everest Base camp Heli tour Start from kathmandu to gateway of Everest region, Lukla. After refeueling at Lukla, you will fly to Everest Base Camp or Kalapathar. Depending upon the weather helicopter flies to Tengboche, Dingboche, Gorakshep, Khumbu glacier and land either at Everest Base camp or Kalapathar. Flight takes you to Lop of Everest and its surrounding peaks. The Awestruck view of the gigantic mountains can be captured. Along the flight you will capture and enjoy the shining vistas of Mt. Everest (8,848m), Mt. Lhotse (8,513m), Mt. Cho oyu (8,153m), Mt. Pumori (7,156m), Mt. Nuptse (7,864m) with many more Himalayan range.

You will be given 5-10 minutes approx. at base camp or kalapathar due to altitude threat, It also provide photogrpaphic opportunities and also a decent chance to take your feet into base camp of world's most elevated pinnacle Mt. Everest. After that, flight will take you to Syangboche at Hotel Everest View for Breakfast, the best hotel in the whole Everest region that provide you the best view of Everest. Then the flight follow back to kathmandu en-route stopping at Lukla for refueling. After fantastic moment with Panoramic Photogrpahy of Himalayas and Breakfast at Hotel Everest view you will be back to kathmandu by Helicopter. After, landed you will pick up by Private Vehicle and If you wish we can even start Kathmandu Sightseeing tour.
