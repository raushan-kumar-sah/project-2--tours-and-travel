NEPAL AND TIBET TOUR

Nepal and Tibet Tour covers all the major highlights that include sightseeing of Kathmandu Durbar Square, Swayambhunath, Bouddhanath Stupa, Pasupatinath temple, Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Pokhara, Chitwan Jungle Safari in Nepal and visit of Sera Monastery, Trupeng Monastery, Norbulingka, and visit The Carpet Factory, Potala Palace, Jokhang Temple, Barkhor Market, Drepung Monastery and much more.

Nepal and Tibet Tour Package start from nepal and unlock with sightseeing of kathmandu vallley which include explore best cultural and historical place Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Kathmandu Durbar Square and Patan Durbar Square. Later on, Explore boudhanath, the largest stupa in south Asia. The Bouddha area is a visual feast. Colorful thangkas, jewellery, hand-woven carpets, and khukuri knives are sold in the surrounding stalls. Pashupatinath temple one of the best hindu temple and Swoyambhunath known as Monkey temple. Outside Valley Explore Pokhara City and Jungle Safari Activities in Chitwan. After the Activites in nepal proceed to Tibet. In Tibet Explore Sera Monastery, Trupeng Monastery, Norbulingka, and visit The Carpet Factory. Sera Monastery It is known as one of the three greatest monasteries of Tibet . Drupeng Monastery, Norbulingka means ” Jeweled Garden “. It was constructed as a summer palace for the Dalai Lama and later served the whole governmental administration. The Carpet Factory, The highland of Tibet is the actual home of the oriental carpets. By visiting Carpet Factory you will have a general idea how carpets are produced. The Potala Palace, Perched upon the Marpo Ri Hill, 130 meters above the Lhasa valley, it is the greatest monumental structure in Tibet . The Jokhang Temple, The Jokhang is the most celebrated temple in Tibet . Because the temple is not controlled by a particular sect of Tibetan Buddhism it attracts adherents of all the sects as well as followers of Bon-Po , Tibet ‘s indigenous religion. Barkhor Street, The Barkhor is the oldest street in Lhasa as well as the center of old Lhasa and remains very traditional. It is a place where Tibetan culture, economy, religion and arts are assembled.

Affordable Nepal and Tibet Tour is available for both group tour basis or private departures so, do let us know when you want to take this tour then we can suggest you some dates.

Day 01: Arrival at Kathmandu airport. Transfer to Hotel.

After arrival and customs clearance in kathmandu airport. You’ll be met by the Plan Nepal tour representative at airport terminal who will be holding Plan Nepal Travel and Tours signboard with your name. we would offer you a warm Nepalese welcome and transferred to Hotel. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 02 : Sightseeing around the Kathmandu valley. Overnight at a hotel.

After Breakfast at hotel, we begin our trip. Explore the historical place Bhaktapur the best cultural and historical place. Later on, we will explore boudhanath, the largest stupa in south Asia. The Bouddha area is a visual feast. Colorful thangkas, jewellery, hand-woven carpets, and khukuri knives are sold in the surrounding stalls. Pashupatinath temple one of the best hindu temple and Swoyambhunath known as Monkey temple. Overnight at hotel.

Day 03 : Drive to Pokhara (900m) via Trishuli River Rafting. Overnight at a hotel.

After Breakfast at hotel, we will proceed our tour to Pokhara via rafting in Trishuli river. On the way encountering the fast flowing river, green forest and have thrilling rafting for 3 hours and drive to Pokhara. Pokhara place of remarkable natural beauty, offers splendid views of the Himalayan ranges. overnight at Hotel.

Day 04 : Guided sightseeing tour in Pokhara. Overnight at a hotel.

After Breakfast Full Day sightseeing of Pokhara Valley including the beautiful Devis Fall, Gupteswar Cave, Mahendra Cave, the International Mountain Museum, Bindya Basini Temple. Overnight at Hotel.

Drive to Sarangkot for Sunrise and Mountain Views and Guided Sightseeing tour of Pokhara or Adventure activities. Overnight at hotel.

Early Morning drive to Sarangkot to see for sunrise view of mountain.Sight-seeing day in Pokhara Valley. Visit world peace pagoda a massive Buddhist stupa and is situated on top of a hill on the southern shore of Phewa lake. visit museums to learn lifestyles and history of ethnic groups such as the Gurung, Thakali and the Tharu are attractively displayed. If you prefer we can do different kind of Adventure sports like Paragliding, Bungee Jumping, Zip Flyer. Overnight at hotel.

Day 06 : Continue Drive to Chitwan National Park. Overnight at a Resort.

Drive to Chitwan national park, the park was declared as UNESCO Heriatge site in 1984. The park includes the deciduous forest, part of churiya hillas, the Rapti, Narayani and reu rivers. With theses you can explore royal bengal tiger,600 plant species, 50 mammals, 526 birds and 49 amphibians, reptiles in the park. You will drive from pokhara to chitwan by the side you will leave the mountainous city pokhara and drive through the plain terai and on the arrival you will be transferred to the hotel. Later on you will get fresh and remainder of the day at your leisure. overnight at resort.

Day 07: Activities in the Chitwan National Park. Overnight at a Resort.

Early morning wake up and Serve tea/coffe and breakfast. Leave from the hotel for Canoe ride along the Rapti River, the canoe ride provides an excited opportunity for Bird Watching and seeing the 2 rare species, Departure for Elephant Safari, this is the best opportunity to see four different kinds of deer, rhinoceros, wild bore, monkey, leopard, sloth bear and the Royal Bengal Tiger (If your luck is favorable). Overnight at resort.

Day 08 : Morning activities in Chitwan, and drive back to Kathmandu. Overnight at a hotel.

Early morning wake up and Serve tea/coffe and breakfast. some extra activities in Chitwan National Park. On these day you will leave chitwan and drive back to kathmandu with the fast flowing trishuli river by your side. Free time on your Own for the trip preperation to tibet. Overnight at hotel.

Day 09 : Flight from Kathmandu to Lhasa. Overnight at a hotel.

Early morning transfer to International Airport for flight to Lhasa. Landing at Gonggar Airport. Warm Welcome by our representative at Airport and Transfer to a hotel in Lhasa (2hrs). Time at leisure. It id advisable to take easy rest for the remainder of day due to Lhasa Altitude. Overnight at hotel in Lhasa.

Day 10 : Guided city tours in Lhasa. Overnight at a hotel.

Today we will have Full day city tour of The Potala Palace, The Jokhang Temple, and Barkhor Street The Potala Palace : Perched upon the Marpo Ri Hill, 130 meters above the Lhasa valley, it is the greatest monumental structure in Tibet . The Jokhang Temple : The Jokhang is the most celebrated temple in Tibet . Because the temple is not controlled by a particular sect of Tibetan Buddhism it attracts adherents of all the sects as well as followers of Bon-Po , Tibet ‘s indigenous religion. Barkhor Street : The Barkhor is the oldest street in Lhasa as well as the center of old Lhasa and remains very traditional. It is a place where Tibetan culture, economy, religion and arts are assembled. Overnight at hotel.

Day 11 : Guided City tour of Lhasa. Overnight at a hotel.

Today we will have Full day city tour of Sera Monastery, Trupeng Monastery, Norbulingka, and visit The Carpet Factory. Sera Monastery It is known as one of the three greatest monasteries of Tibet . Drupeng Monastery : It is one of the biggest monasteries in Tibet . Norbulingka : Norbulingka means ” Jeweled Garden “. It was constructed as a summer palace for the Dalai Lama and later served the whole governmental administration. The Carpet Factory : The highland of Tibet is the actual home of the oriental carpets. By visiting Carpet Factory you will have a general idea how carpets are produced. Overnight at hotel.

Day 12 : Free day to explore at your leisure on Lhasa. Overnight at a hotel.
Day 13 : Flight to Kathmandu from Lhasa. Overnight at a hotel.

In the morning after breakfast, transfer to airport 02 hrs. Drive-Representative will assist you. Flight Lhasa/Kathmandu lasts for an hour. During the flight you will see the various beauties likeYarlung Tsangpo River , Lake Yamdrok , Mt. Kanchenjunga (8586m) and Mt. Makalu (8463m) and Mt.Everest (8848m). It too depends upon the weather. Upon arrival at Kathmandu aiport pick up and transfer to Hotel. Free day on your own for shopping, take relax or stroll around the street of thamel. In evening there will be welcome dinner hosted by Plan Nepal tour. we offer you a Farewell dinner in one of the finest cultural restaurant in nepal which will introduce you to the country's food. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 14 : Transfer to the International airport for final departure from Kathmandu.

We will exchange our good-byes and now you will be leaving Nepal with all the good memories and unforgettable Experience. We know and hope that you will always remember this Nepal Tibet Multi Country tour Package and after return home tell stories of your amazing experiences in Nepal with Plan Nepal. we will provide a ride to International airport for final depature as per your flight schedule.
